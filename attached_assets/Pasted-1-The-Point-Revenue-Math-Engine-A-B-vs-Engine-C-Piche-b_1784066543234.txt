Hum user portal se "Vault" ya "Locked Points" ka lafz hi mita denge taake user ko dukh na ho ke us ke paise phanse hue hain. Hum Vault (15\%) aur Bonus (20\%) ko piche background me merge kar ke unhe aik single naam de dete hain: "Guild Weekly Bonus Pool" (35\%).
