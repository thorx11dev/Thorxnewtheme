### CATEGORY 1: Mistakes, Bugs, and Gaps (Expectation vs. Reality)
Identify every single feature that is half-baked, buggy, or does not behave exactly as a real user expects:
* **The Points-only Illusion**: Where in the frontend (graphs, tables, dashboard widgets, history, notifications) does the real PKR balance leak? It must ONLY show TX-Points until the withdrawal screen.

### CATEGORY 2: The "Million-Dollar Company" Standards Gap
Identify what is missing to make this a enterprise-grade, bulletproof system:
* **Security & Authentication**: Audit all admin/team routes. Are there places missing `requireSessionAuth`? Are crucial routes (like withdrawal creation and password resets) missing rate limiters (`rate-limit`)?

### CATEGORY 3: Ecosystem Disconnection & UX Friction

* **UX/UI Polish**: List every spot where loading states are just ugly text ("...") instead of smooth skeleton screens, or where mutations (like copy/share) fail silently without showing a beautiful toast notification to the user.
